Welcome to Pause

A small wellness corner for the Lillio team. Built because Headspace got expensive and your day still needs a few quiet minutes.

01

Pick the moment, not the topic

Got 60 seconds before circle time? Under 3 minutes. Mid-morning slump? Quick break. End of a long day? Sleep & wind down. The sidebar is built around when you'll use it, not what it's called.

02

Press play, that's the whole thing

Every video and playlist plays right here in the page. No accounts, no sign-in. Spotify gives 30-second previews if you don't have a Spotify account, full playback if you do.

03

Use the timer for actual breaks

The Pomodoro timer is a 25 / 5 with a longer break every fourth round. Tweak the intervals to whatever fits your day. Pair it with focus music for deep work.

04

Save what you love, come back tomorrow

Tap the heart on any card to save it. Your saved list lives in your browser. The library refreshes every Monday with new picks.

Got a favorite video or playlist that should be in here? DM Vahid on Slack and I'll add it. Same for new categories, broken videos, or anything else. This is for you.

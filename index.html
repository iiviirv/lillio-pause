Welcome to Lillio Pause

A small wellness break, on us. Here's how to make the most of it.

1

Pick a tab that matches the moment

Five minutes between activities? Quick Resets. Lesson planning at the desk? Focus Music. End of a long day? Wind Down. There's no wrong choice — these are just shortcuts.

2

Press play — that's it

Every video and playlist plays right here in the page. You don't need to sign in to YouTube or Spotify (Spotify gives you 30-second previews without an account; full playback if you have one). Headphones recommended, but not required.

3

Use the Timer for real breaks

The Timer tab is a Pomodoro: 25 minutes of focus, 5 minutes off, and a longer break every fourth round. Tweak the intervals to whatever fits your day. Pair it with focus music for deep work, or let it nudge you toward a real break.

4

Come back tomorrow

The library refreshes weekly with fresh meditations and music. Bookmark this page or pin it in your browser — it'll always be a click away when you need a beat.
